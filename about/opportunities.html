Career Oportunities

DataSense Solutions is always looking for talented consultants and instructors. If you have strong business intelligence experience and excellent interpersonal skills, please send your resume to us at jobs@datasensesolutions.com.

Currently, DataSense is actively recruiting for the following position(s):

BI and DW Consultants and Trainers

The ideal consulting candidate should have as many of the following:

  • Hands-on experience with one or more of the following Business Objects product:
    • Business Objects
    • WebIntelligence
    • Designer
    • Supervisor
    • Crystal Reports
    • Data Integrator
    • ACTA
    • First Logic
    • Infomersion/Xcelsius
    • Dashboard Manager
    • Performance Manager
  • Experience with competitive tools like Informatica, Cognos, Brio, Hyperion, or other BI and DW tools
  • Prior consulting experience with BI, ETL and DW is a plus but not mandatory
  • Knowledge of relational databases and SQL
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • An undergraduate or graduate degree

Depending on your background you will be expected to perform the following activities:

  • Project management activities
  • Gathering and analyzing business requirements
  • Installing and configuring software
  • Defining and delivering solutions to support business needs
  • Delivering test plans and testing applications
  • Delivering knowledge transfer and formal training sessions

*Majority of work is in the Boston area but occasional travel may be required.

Please send resumes to jobs@datasensesolutions.com
