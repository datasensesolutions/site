DataSense Training Services

DataSense Solutions is an authorized SAP/Business Objects training provider in the Boston and New England area. We provide training for SAP BI, Business Objects XI, Crystal Reports/Crystal Enterprise and classic Business Objects product lines. Our highly qualified trainers have extensive business and technical backgrounds, providing a deeply insightful base for helping students master the skills they require.

Our public training facilities are in Waltham, MA, just 11 miles from Boston and easily accessible via I-90 and 128/95.

In addition to our public courses, we also offer a wide array of on site, virtual and custom education services for our clients. Contact us today to discuss options for your private or custom training needs.
