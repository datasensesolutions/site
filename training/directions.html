Cancellation, rescheduling, and refund processes for classroom and virtual classroom cancellations and rescheduling:

Classroom and virtual classroom cancellations and rescheduling will only be processed by calling our office directly at +1-781-487-2625 X200.

We will confirm all cancellations or rescheduling requests by e-mail. Financial responsibility remains with the customer for all class fees unless a proper cancellation or rescheduling request is received and confirmed prior to the start of the class.

The following classroom cancellation and rescheduling fee schedule applies:

  • More than 11 business - days no charge
  • 4 - 10 business days - 50% of the total class fee
  • 3 or fewer business days - 100% of the total class fee

*Date of cancellation or rescheduling request (measured from the first day of the class) Applicable fees.
